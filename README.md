Super Sudsy is a fun, action-packed 2D platformer where you play as Reh Dogg, the freshest superhero in the city! Armed with a mighty toilet brush and a flowing sudsy cape, you must leap across rooftops, battle grimy enemies, and clean up the streets of a neon-lit urban nightscape.
Game Features:
•  Epic Hero — Play as Reh Dogg with smooth movement and silky smooth animations
•  Unique Enemies — Face Patrol Goons, aggressive Chasers, Shooters with homing projectiles, flying Bombers, and tough Tanks
•  Power-ups — Collect soap bottles for temporary speed boosts and big score bonuses
•  Challenging Gameplay — Precise platforming, enemy stomping, and projectile dodging
•  Atmosphere — Vibrant dark urban aesthetic with glowing buildings and floating bubbles
•  Sound & Music — Retro-style sound effects and energetic background music
•  Multiple Screens — Polished Title Screen and Game Over with high score tracking
Can you help Reh Dogg defeat the forces of grime and become the ultimate champion of cleanliness?
Controls:
•  A / D or Arrow Keys — Move
•  Space — Jump
Genre: 2D Platformer, Action, Retro-Inspired
